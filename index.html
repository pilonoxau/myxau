Trading Rules

Clear, structured trading rules to maintain discipline and consistency

Session Rules

Market
Trade only XAUUSD (Gold) for optimal liquidity and volatility
Trading Sessions
London Session: 08:00 - 16:00 GMT | New York Session: 13:00 - 21:00 GMT
Maximum Trades Per Day
Maximum 3 trades per day to prevent overtrading

Risk Management

Risk Per Trade
Risk exactly 2.5% of your current account balance per trade
Maximum Daily Loss
Stop trading when daily loss reaches 7.5% of account balance
Never Move Stop Loss
Set SL and never move it. Let the trade play out.
Never Revenge Trade
After a loss, don't increase lot size. Stick to the plan.

Entry Rules

1H Trend
EMA50 above EMA100 = BUY only | EMA50 below EMA100 = SELL only
15M Confirmation
Price must be aligned with EMA50 on 15M timeframe
3M Pullback
Wait for a pullback to key support/resistance level
1M Entry
Enter only after a clear entry candle confirms the move

Exit Rules

Stop Loss Placement
Place SL below the previous swing low (BUY) or above previous swing high (SELL)
Take Profit
Target 4x your risk (1:4 Reward:Risk ratio)
Partial Takes
Take 50% at 2R, move SL to breakeven, let rest run to 4R

Stop Trading Rules

After 3 Consecutive Losses
Immediately stop trading for the remainder of the day
Daily Loss Limit
When daily loss reaches 7.5% of balance, stop all trading
Emotional State
If feeling anxious, revengeful, or emotional, stop trading
Market Conditions
Stop trading during high-impact news events or extreme volatility
